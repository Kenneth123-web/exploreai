What is AI?

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a branch of computer science that focuses on creating intelligent machines capable of performing tasks that typically require human intelligence.

Types of AI

Narrow AI

Specialized in specific tasks, such as language translation or facial recognition.

General AI

Has the ability to perform any intellectual task that a human can do.

Superintelligent AI

Goes beyond human capabilities, potentially transforming industries and society.

The Future of AI

AI is shaping the future, with advancements in robotics, healthcare, education, and more. Explore its potential to revolutionize the way we live and work.

The future of AI will include developments like autonomous systems, personalized medicine, and innovations in education and communication.
